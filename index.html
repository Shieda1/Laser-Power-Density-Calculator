What is the laser power density?
The power density is the ratio of power in watt to the cross section area.
How to calculate laser power density?
To calculate laser power density you should use the following formula:
power density = p / A
where:
p = power
a = cross section area
Why use laser power density?
The laser power density is used in order to dtermine whether a beam will damage an optic or detector.
